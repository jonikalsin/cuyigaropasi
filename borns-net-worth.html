The singer started performing live as a support voice for Mister Wives on their tour entitled “Our Own House Tour”, before in 2015 his debut studio album “Dopamine” was released, which climbed to second place on the Billboard Alt and Rock charts. In the middle of the same year, he was the star of the evening at the Electrowerkz club in London, and was then accompanied by Charli XCX and Bleachers on their “Charli and Jack Do America Tour”, including at the Lollapalooza festival in Chicago. He also performed at the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival the same year.
