Flying Without Wings
Dub M02 ED 03
ArtistWestlife
LyricsSteve Mac, Wayne Hector
ComposerSteve Mac, Wayne Hector

Flying Without Wings is one of the ending themes for the English dub of The Power of One. It is featured on the film's soundtrack and is performed by Westlife.
