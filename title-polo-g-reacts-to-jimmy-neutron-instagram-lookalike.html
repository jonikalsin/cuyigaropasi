Polo G Reacts To 'Jimmy Neutron' Instagram Lookalike

June 10, 2024
blog

Polo G is pretty active when it comes to using his social media channels. One thing it seems he likes to do is take part in trends that are dominating apps like Instagram and TikTok.

On Wednesday (February 2), Polo G took to his Instagram and took part in the infamous “Before/After” celebrity lookalike trend where people take a photo of their doppelgänger and place it over their likeness. Polo G decided to have some fun and clown himself by using a picture of Libby Folfax, a character from the popular early 2000s Nickelodeon show The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron, Boy Genius that looks eerily similar to Polo with their matching braided hair.

“They Got Me Fucked up,” Polo G wrote in the caption of his Instagram post.

Several of Polo’s celebrity peers chimed in on the hilarious video with crying laughing emojis such as Guapdad 4000, Gillie The Kid, Iman Shumpert and Hitman Holla. Others actually left comments under the clip to go along with all the laughter.
