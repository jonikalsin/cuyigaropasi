Jets fans, we’re just a few days away from the beginning of the much-awaited Aaron Rodgers era.

Starting Thursday, Aug. 3, the Gang Green will kick off their four-game preseason against the Cleveland Browns at Canton, OH’s Tom Benson Hall of Fame Stadium.

After that, Robert Saleh’s squad head southeast for a matchup against the Carolina Panthers at Charlotte, NC’s Bank of America Stadium on Saturday, Aug. 12.

The following week, Sauce Gardner and co. play at their home MetLife Stadium for the first time on Saturday, Aug. 19 when they take on the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

Finally, they wrap their preseason run against their cross-conference rivals, the New York Giants on Saturday, Aug. 26.
