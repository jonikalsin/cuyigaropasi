Patty Jenkins is not a strange name in giving blockbuster movies. She is directing the films like Wonder Woman and its upcoming part, Wonder Woman 1984. How Much Is The Net Worth Of Patty Jenkins?

Patty Jenkins, who stands at the height of 5 feet and 7 inches tall, has a net worth of $25 million, which she garners as an American film director and writer. It's been nearly a couple of decades since she started directing the entertainment production in the industry. As a director, she directed several movies and tv shows, including Velocity Rules (2001), Arrested Development (2004), Five (2011), The Killing (2011-12), Betrayal (2013), Wonder Woman (2017), and others.

Similarly, her upcoming credits are Epilogue: Etta's Mission (2017), I Am the Night (2019), Wonder Woman 1984 (2020), and Cleopatra.

Patty Jenkins is also a recipient of the 2012 DGA Awards for Outstanding Directorial Achievement in Dramatic Series, which she won for 'The Killing.' Similarly, she won the Outstanding Directorial Achievement in Movies for Television/Mini-Series for Five.

Who Is Patty Jenkins Married To?

Patty Jenkins, age 49, is married to her long-term husband, Sam Sheridan, a film writer and producer. As a writer, his accolades include SEAL Team (2017), and I Am The Night (2019). Similarly, Sheridan served as a firefighter for the Forest Service based in the Gila National Forest, New Mexico, from 2003 to 2004.

Patty Jenkins and her husband, Sam Sheridan (©: Zimbio)

The lovebirds said, 'I do' back on 1 September 2007 at the Presidio Chapel in Santa Barbara, California. The minister of the United Church of Christ, Rev. Dr. Ronald T. Evans, officiated Jenkins and Sheridan's wedding ceremony.

Though the lovebirds have crossed a decade-long married life milestone, they do not have any children. Nevertheless, there is no second thought that they relish a peaceful and soothing romance, as of now.
